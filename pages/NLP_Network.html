Networks and Force System

Network visualization that shows the relationships between each word in 'The Adventures of Sherlock Holmes - Arthur Conan Doyle' book. Both the semantic and the closeness relations of the words are taken into account. The network self-organizes using the force system of d3.js to find interesting topologies and paths between the data.
